ULITRA STARTER PACK (No developer needed)

What you do:
1) Upload EVERYTHING inside this folder to your GitHub repo (Arunphalke/ulitra).
2) Then connect Netlify to that repo.
3) Your website becomes live.

Important:
- /admin is your product manager (Decap CMS)
- Products live in /products (auto-created by admin)
- Images live in /images (uploaded by admin)

Default categories: Beauty, Gadgets, Fashion, Home
